PatientCare

Patients trust Dr. Hyman for all of their primary healthcare needs, including how to stay well and meet the physical demands of their careers and family life.

ExecuCare

Executives turn to their Chief Health Advisor, Dr. Hyman, for both executive physicals using the latest technology, and ongoing healthcare and wellness counsel.

PilotCare

Dr. Hyman is certified to provide pilots with annual Class 2 and 3 medical exams required by the FAA for all pilots, and offers ongoing care as well.

TravelCare

Dr. Hyman is also a travel medicine specialist who is certified by the California Department of Health to provide yellow fever vaccines, and equipped with other preventive care treatments for international travel.

Welcome to HymanHealth

Dear patients,

The world of medicine and patient care is experiencing turbulence. Recent healthcare and insurance reform are creating uncertainties. At the same time, this is an era of exciting medical breakthroughs and technological developments enabling physicians to help patients with preventive care and disease management more than ever.

At our office, we are constantly evolving to stay one step ahead of these changes, ensuring that we continue to provide exceptional care to every patient. Many of you know that I continue to teach at UCLA Medical School. I want to share my latest insights with you on a regular basis, so we have created a password-protected section on our Website with my summary and analysis of the latest medical findings that may apply to the health of you or a loved one.

In addition, we have invested in the latest diagnostic technology because we understand the importance of discovering potential health problems before they escalate.  We are now offering an Executive Health program, ExecuCare, which includes both executive physicals and ongoing care by our practice. And for those of you who are pilots, I have been approved by the FAA to provide annual medical examinations to non-commercial pilots. And for travelers, remember that HymanHealth is approved by the Center for Disease Control for yellow fever vaccinations and is able to manage all of your travel medicine needs.

Finally, we continue to enhance our offices to make sure you are comfortable and at ease during your visits. We respect you as individuals and will continue our record of short wait times. I strive to be highly engaged with patients. My staff and I are well known to call you back promptly. Please let me know if we are not meeting your needs.

My practice has grown through referrals from patients and I appreciate your trust in me enough to spread the word to others in your life. Thank you and I look forward to seeing you at your next visit.

Sincerely,
Mark H. Hyman, MD
