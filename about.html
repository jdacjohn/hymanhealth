Practice Overview

HymanHealth is an internal medical practice headed by Dr. Mark H. Hyman, who has been providing patients on the the Westside of Los Angeles with whole health guidance and extraordinary care for more than 20 years. Executives, families, retirees, entertainers, pilots and professional athletes alike turn to our office for all of their primary healthcare needs – from executive and flight physicals, travel medicine and preventive care, to disease management, and treatment of illnesses and injuries. They also trust Dr. Hyman for counsel on how to stay well and meet the physical demands of their careers and family life.    

At the heart of our office is a respect for patient privacy and care, coupled with Dr. Hyman’s pursuit of continuous improvements in medicine.  In our offices, patients will find a plethora of the latest diagnostic medical equipment, along with helpful handouts and Dr. Hyman’s books and articles, many published in medical journals. It is this duality of compassion for patients and commitment to excellence in medicine that has earned our practice its good reputation and Web MD’s recent recognition of Dr. Hyman as a “Health Hero.”
