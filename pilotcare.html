HymanHealth PilotCare

Program Overview
FAA-certified exams

HymanHealth is an internal medical practice headed by Dr. Mark H. Hyman, who has been providing patients on the Westside with extraordinary care for more than 20 years. Dr. Hyman is certified by the Federal Aviation Authority (FAA) to conduct the required annual physicals to non-commercial pilots. Student and private pilots are welcome to receive their FAA Class 2 and 3 physical exams at HymanHealth.

With our office centrally located on the Westside of Los Angeles, we offer fast, efficient and convenient exams near where you live, fly, and work. The exams are conducted in the privacy of Dr. Hyman’s office. His office contains the most extensive in-office advanced screening equipment of any practice or clinic on the Westside – even universities - and several unique state-of-science testing machines not found elsewhere. Should you desire a more comprehensive “360 degree” diagnostic physical and ongoing care, including the added benefits of our PilotCare Plus service, let us know. Aeromedical Examinations are a necessary part of flying, so why not consider integrating this exam with your ongoing primary health care.

Executives, entertainers, pilots and professional athletes alike turn to our office for all of their primary healthcare needs – from executive and flight physicals, travel medicine and preventive care, to disease management, and treatment of illnesses and injuries. They also trust Dr. Hyman for counsel on how to stay well and meet the physical demands of their careers and family life.   

In our offices, patients will find a plethora of helpful handouts and Dr. Hyman’s books and articles, many published in medical journals. It is this combination of compassion for patients and commitment to excellence in medicine that has earned our practice its good reputation and Web MD’s recent recognition of Dr. Hyman as a “Health Hero.”

Contact us at 310 243-0400 for more information on Flight Exams.
